# Expansion Standard

CivilizationX can grow to 324+ entries only if the new information improves survival, repair, teaching, and verification. Do not quadruple the Codex with empty catalog pages.

## Minimum Bar For New Entries

Every added technology should include:

- A concrete problem statement explaining what failure it prevents.
- A practical overview with collapse-recovery and space-settlement implications.
- At least 3 principles, 5 components, 3 raw material groups, and 5 build or operating steps.
- Warnings on every medical, fire, electrical, chemical, biological, structural, pressure, sharp-tool, confined-space, or high-temperature step.
- At least 3 credible sources, with institutional or textbook sources preferred over Wikipedia.
- A clear maturity label. Medical and high-risk entries should stay `review-needed` until qualified review exists.

## Life-Critical Rule

If bad instructions could injure or kill someone, the entry must make escalation limits obvious. It should explain what the technology is for, when it is not enough, what must be measured exactly, and what needs professional verification.

4. Promote maturity only after the entry meets the field-guide depth standard.
