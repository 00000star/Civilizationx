# CivilizationX Master Plan

CivilizationX is a civilization bootstrap manual for two mirror scenarios:

- Collapse recovery: rebuilding core capabilities when modern institutions and supply chains fail.
- Space settlement: building, repairing, and substituting technologies away from Earth supply chains.

The project should grow as an offline-first, source-backed, safety-aware technology graph.

## Product Pillars

1. The Tree: dependency map of human technology.
2. The Documentary: deep, structured pages for each technology.
3. The Atlas: raw materials, Earth locations, space substitutes, and material flows.
4. The Pathfinder: target-oriented routes through prerequisites.
5. The Review System: verification, hazard classification, source quality, and expert review.
