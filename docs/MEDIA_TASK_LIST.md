## Current Media Standard

Every serious entry should eventually have:

- At least one overview image.
- At least one process diagram.
- At least one component diagram where the technology has named parts.
- At least one real-world photo or generated field illustration.
- License/credit metadata recorded in the entry JSON.

Generated images are acceptable when:

- They are clearly credited as generated or project-created media.
- They illustrate concepts, systems, or process flows without pretending to be documentary evidence.
- They do not replace real photos where real photos are needed for identification or inspection.
